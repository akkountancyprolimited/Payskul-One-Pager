⚠ The Problem
  • Millions of Nigerian families live paycheck-to-paycheck, unable to pay school fees on time
  • Schools lose revenue and stability due to unpaid or delayed fee collection
  • Students face suspension, withdrawal, and interrupted education
  • No accessible, structured financial solution exists for the mass market
✦ Our Solution
  • BNPL model: parents pay school fees now, repay in flexible installments
  • Schools receive 100% upfront payment — zero collection risk
  • Mobile-first app with seamless onboarding via Mono & Pecan MFB integration
  • Win-win-win for students, parents, and schools across Nigeria and beyond
